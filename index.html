What is

Main Street Certified Vendors

Vendor IQ evaluates the service, product quality, and prices of each vendor, and surveys hundreds of restaurants in each market to establish vendor ratings. Main Street Certified Vendors consistently offer great service, fair pricing, and high product quality.

Optimized Vendor Matching

Our system matches a restaurant's profile with local Main Street Certified Vendors to ensure the most strategic and compatible partnership.

Worry-Free Pricing

Main Street Certified Vendors will meet or beat the prices of current vendors before you place your first order. Certified Vendors provide purchasing data and we actively monitor prices to ensure that price fluctuations are consistent with the market.

How It Works

  • evaluates the entire vendor market for you.

    Not all vendors are created equal. Vendor IQ gathers real, unbiased feedback to rate vendors in your area. Finally you’ll know which vendors offer great service, quality products, and fair pricing to main street restaurants.

  • refers you certified vendors that fit your unique preferences and needs.

    With hundreds of vendors fighting for your business, how do you know which is a good fit? We focus on what makes your restaurant unique while filtering through real-time data and insights to match you with not just a great vendor, but the right vendor.

  • serves as your 'watchdog'

    Once you’ve found a vendor you love, receive the transparency you need to remain confident and worry-free. Vendor IQ works with certified vendors to monitor your prices and market trends, ensuring you’re always receiving top-notch service and excellent pricing.
